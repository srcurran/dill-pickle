Consulting Services

Dillingham Consulting acts as an advisor to any company in the residential real estate/mortgage lending profession that is looking to solve for issues relating to possible/existing/identified issues/risks/regulatory shortfalls and is looking for a solution that involves issue identification and training.

Company Training

Dillingham Consulting LLC works with clients to determine the impact of, and assist in the creation of, training to educate the client’s employees, customers, and other business partners in various disciplines related to residential mortgage lending, mortgage banking compliance; federal and state regulatory compliance; financial services law and real estate law

Ruth Dillingham applies her decades of legal training both in the areas of established banking law and federal regulations as well as her on-going monitoring of new and revised laws and regulations to provide comprehensive training programs. In the course of her work with her clients, Ruth is available to analyze legal issues arising from new or existing laws and regulations and/or their applicability as determined by new regulator opinion or judicial decisions. Based on her experience, she can evaluate the impact of a change in regulation or law on operations and services and provide advice or recommendations as needed. In creating a training program Ruth’s services include:

Training Design: Based on the collaboration with the client to assess needs, Ruth will create a training proposal including recommended delivery method, content areas, and timing, utilizing adult learning techniques.

Training Development: Once the training is designed and agreed to, Ruth will develop training materials for delivery through web-based or instructor-led training methods including attendee and instructor materials, and teaching and job aids, using presentation software (e.g. PowerPoint) and word processing software (e.g. Word).

Training Delivery: After client approval of the training program, Ruth will coordinate with the client’s and conduct the training for large groups or individuals online or onsite.
